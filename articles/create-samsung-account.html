How to Create a Samsung Account

Learn how to create a Samsung accoount for free online.


Create an Accoount Online

  1. Visit account.samsung.com
  2. Click Create Account in the top right corner of the page.
  3. After agreeing to the Terms and Conditions Agreement, enter your personal information.
  4. Setup Two-Step Verification to add a layer of security to your account
  5. Verify your email to activate your account with the email sent to your inbox

You must be at least 13 years old to create a Samsung account without parental or guardian help and supervision. The minimum age varies depending on what country you are located in.


Create an Account on a Samsung Galaxy Phone or Tablet

  1. Open Settings
  2. Scroll down and tap Manage Accounts
  3. Tap Add Account
  4. Tap Samsung Account
  5. Tap Create Account
  6. Follow the on-screen steps to create an account.

You must be at least 13 years old to create a Samsung account without parental or guardian help and supervision. The minimum age varies depending on what country you are located in.
