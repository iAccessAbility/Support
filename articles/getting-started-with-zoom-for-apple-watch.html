Getting Started with the Accessibility Zoom Feature for Apple Watch

The Zoom feature on Apple Watch allows a user to magnify anything on the display no matter what you are viewing. This article will go over how to setup, enable, use, and disable Zoom.


Locate and Enable

  1. Open Settings
  2. Tap Accessibility
  3. Tap Zoom
  4. Enable Zoom

Use Zoom

  • Double-tap the screen with two figners to zoom in and out
  • Double-tap and swipe up and down with two fingers to adjust the magnification.
  • Drag with two fingers around the screen to move the magnification window around.
  • Tap once with two fingers to switch the Digital Crown between panning with Zoom and normal use.

Zoom Settings

There are a couple settings you can adjust for Zoom listed and explained below:

  • Maximum Zoom Level: Set the max magnification you can perform with Zoom.
  • Hand Gestures: Use Zoom with Hand Gestures instead of directly tapping and swiping on the display. Learn how to set it up below.

Setup Hand Gestures

  1. Open Settings
  2. Tap Accessibillity
  3. Tap Zoom
  4. Tap Hand Gestures
  5. Set the various gestures to Zoom actions.
  6. Enable Hand Gestures

The normal gestures still work when Hand Gestures are enabled.

Note that on supported models, the universal double-tap gesture is unavailable when Hand Gestures is enabled for Zoom.


You can setup Accessibility Shortcut to quickly and easily toggle on Zoom or other Accessibility features. Learn how to setup Accessibility Shortcut here: Setup and Use Accessbility Shortcut >
