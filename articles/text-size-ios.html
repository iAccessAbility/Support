Adjust the Text Size on iPhone, iPad, and iPod touch

Learn how to adjust the Text Size on iPhone, iPad, and iPod touch.


Change the Text Size

  1. Open Settings
  2. Tap Display & Brightness
  3. Tap Text Size
  4. Drag the slider to adjust the text size.

Make the Text Size Larger

You can enable accessible text sizes to enlarge the text size even more.

  1. Open Settings
  2. Tap Accessibility
  3. Tap Display & Text Size
  4. Top Larger Text
  5. Enable Larger Text
  6. Drag the slider to the right increase the text size more.

Control Center

You can also add the Text Size setting to Control Center to easily adjust while in apps or on websites. Learn how to add it here: Access, Use, and Customize the Control Center on Your iPhone, iPad, and iPod touch >
